Branding Guidelines

Find guides and downloads to help you get started with the Palette brand.

Palette aims to promote sustainability by creating a grocery line that makes smaller food portions more accessible.

Palette is a grocery company that sells everyday food items in smaller portions. Targeted towards single-person households, Palette aims to reduce food waste by portioning foods for only one person. With the smaller servings, shoppers are also encouraged to try and experiment different food items without needing to commit to bulk sizes.

The Palette logo features different food items arranged in a circular manner to symbolize food on a plate.


The dynamic identity symbolizes the various possibilities with the different ingredients. Everyone’s “palette” is different. The possibilities are endless.

Typeface embodies the circular shape to resemble a meal plate. The rounded san-serif aesthetic reflects the fun, light-hearted tone of the Palette brand. We want to brand cooking as a fun experience rather than an intimidating one.
